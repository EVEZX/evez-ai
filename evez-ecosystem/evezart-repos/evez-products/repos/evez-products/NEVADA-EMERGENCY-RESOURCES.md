# Emergency Resources — Laughlin, Nevada (Clark County)

## IMMEDIATE CASH / HOUSING

### Nevada 211
- **Dial 2-1-1** from any phone — connects to ALL local assistance programs
- Available 24/7
- Ask for: emergency housing, rental assistance, food, cash assistance

### Clark County Social Service
- **Phone: 702-455-0000**
- Emergency shelter, general assistance, rental assistance
- 517 E. Russell Rd, Las Vegas (70 miles from Laughlin — they can refer locally)

### HELP of Southern Nevada (Boulder City — 30 min from Laughlin)
- **Phone: 702-369-4357**
- Emergency rent/mortgage assistance
- Utility assistance (LIHEAP)
- Food pantry
- **CLOSEST major assistance to Laughlin**

### Catholic Charities of Southern Nevada
- **Phone: 702-385-3384**
- Emergency shelter, meals, clothing
- No religious requirement

### Salvation Army — Las Vegas
- **Phone: 702-870-4430**
- Emergency rent/utility assistance
- Shelter

### Laughlin-Specific
- **Laughlin Nevada Community** — check with local churches for emergency aid
- **Mohave County, AZ** (just across the river, 5 min away) — they sometimes serve Laughlin residents
  - Mohave County Community Action: **928-753-0718**

## FOOD

### SNAP (Food Stamps) — APPLY ONLINE
- https://dwss.nv.gov/SNAP/
- Expedited approval if homeless (7 days or less)
- Apply from phone — no office visit needed
- **Call: 1-800-992-0900**

### Food Banks Near Laughlin
- **River Valley Community Food Bank** — Bullhead City, AZ (5 min)
- **Three Square Food Bank** — Las Vegas (serves all Clark County)

## MEDICAL

### Nevada Medicaid
- **Apply online:** https://dwss.nv.gov/Medicaid/
- Homeless = expedited enrollment
- Covers: doctor, hospital, prescriptions, mental health

### Community Health Centers
- **First Southern Nevada Community Health Center** — accepts Medicaid, sliding scale for uninsured

## PHONE / INTERNET

### Lifeline (Free Phone Service)
- **Apply:** https://www.lifelinesupport.org/
- Free smartphone + unlimited talk/text + data if you qualify (SNAP/Medicaid/homeless)
- Steven likely already qualifies

### Affordable Connectivity Program
- $30/mo toward internet service
- May be available through Lifeline enrollment
